Questions.

Answers.

Is it really free with unlimited habits?

Yes. We believe habit tracking only works when you can track everything. The free tier has no habit cap, ever. Pro unlocks analytics, themes, and custom icons — extras for power users who want to go deeper.

Does the widget work without opening the app?

Yes. Ritual uses WidgetKit interactive widgets (iOS 17+), so you can tap a habit directly on your home screen and it marks complete — no app launch required.

What Apple Watch features are included free?

The full Watch app is free: view your habits, mark them complete, and see today's progress — all from your wrist. One tap, instant sync to your iPhone.

Does Ritual sync across devices?

Habits are stored locally using SwiftData. Optional iCloud sync keeps your devices in step — entirely private to you, with no server in the middle.

What data do you collect?

Nothing personal. No account required, no analytics SDK, no data sold. All habits live on-device. See our privacy policy for full details.

Is Ritual a medical or clinical tool?

No. Ritual is a personal productivity app for habit tracking. It is not a medical or clinical tool. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for health, mental health, or behavioral guidance.
