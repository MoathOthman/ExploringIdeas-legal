How it works

Pick a hero

Choose a character and give it a name — "Maya the Brave Fox" — and they star in every story.

Tap to choose

A warm voice reads every line aloud. At each turning point, your child taps a big glowing choice.

Grow the world

Finish a story and a new place lights up on the Story Map — a whole world that grows as they read.

🔒

Private by default

No account. No ads. No analytics. No location. StoryNest collects nothing — the App Store label is "Data Not Collected." A real parental gate stands in front of anything that isn't a story.
