Questions & answers.

Do I need an account?

No. Hush requires no account, no sign-in, and no email address. Open it and play.

Does it really play with the screen locked?

Yes. Hush uses iOS background audio so it continues playing when you lock your phone, switch apps, or put the phone face-down.

Is the free tier actually free forever?

Yes. There is no trial clock. All 10 sounds are free to play individually, forever. Pro adds the 4-layer mixer and sleep timer.

Will it help with tinnitus?

Many people find white, pink, or brown noise helpful for masking tinnitus. Hush is not a medical device and this is not medical advice — please consult a doctor for tinnitus concerns.

What happens to my lifetime purchase if I get a new phone?

Pro is tied to your Apple ID via App Store. Sign into the same Apple ID on your new device, open Hush, and tap Restore Purchases.
