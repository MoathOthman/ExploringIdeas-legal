Support & FAQ

Need help? Email dark2torch@gmail.com — we aim to reply within two business days. Common questions below.

Is my training data safe?

Yes. GymLedger has no servers for your data and requires no account. Every set, rep, weight, and routine is stored on your device and is never uploaded anywhere. Read the full Privacy Policy.

Does GymLedger sync to the cloud?

Not in this version. GymLedger is local-first: your data lives on your phone. Because there is no cloud sync yet, we recommend keeping your own backup of anything important — uninstalling the app removes its data.

Does it connect to Apple Health?

Not in this version. GymLedger does not use HealthKit today. It's something we may add later — if we do, we'll update our Privacy Policy first and explain exactly what health data is used.

Does it work offline?

Entirely. Logging, routines, history, and bodyweight all work offline. Your data is processed on your device, so no connection is needed.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds for any in-app purchase are handled by Apple — visit reportaproblem.apple.com. We have no mechanism to issue refunds directly.
