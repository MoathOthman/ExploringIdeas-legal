Is my data private?

Yes. No account, no servers. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.

Does it use Apple Health?

Only if you turn on the weight trend, and only to read your weight — FastLine never writes to Health.

Do I have to pay?

No. The timer and all stats are free forever. Pro is optional.

Is fasting safe?

FastLine is an educational tracking tool, not medical advice. Fasting isn't right for everyone — talk to a doctor before making changes.
