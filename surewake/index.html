Is my data private?

Yes. No account, no servers. Your alarms stay on your device. See our Privacy Policy.

Will the alarm fire if my phone is on silent?

iOS limits what any app can do against the silent switch. The reliability checklist inside the app explains your exact setup. We are transparent about limits — not because we want to, but because honesty is better than a 1-star review.

What is a dismiss mission?

A short task you must complete to turn off the alarm — math problems, shaking the phone, or walking 20 steps. It forces you to wake up before dismissing.

Do I have to pay?

No. One alarm with the math mission is free forever. Pay once for unlimited alarms and all missions.
