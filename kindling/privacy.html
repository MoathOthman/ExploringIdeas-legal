Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

The short version: Kindling collects no data at all. There is no account, no login, no analytics, and no tracking. Everything you do in Kindling — your answers, your streak, your anniversaries, your history — is stored only on your device and never leaves your phone. Nothing is ever sent to us or to anyone else. The only thing that involves a third party is a purchase, which is processed by Apple or Google, not by us.

Data we collect: none

Kindling does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. There is no Kindling account, no Kindling server, and no Kindling database. We have no way of knowing who you are, that you use the app, or what you and your partner answer. To be explicit:

  • We do not require or ask for an email address, phone number, name, or any login.
  • We do not use any analytics or usage-tracking SDK.
  • We do not use crash-reporting tools that transmit data off your device.
  • We do not use advertising, ad networks, the advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID), or cross-app tracking.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, location, microphone, camera, or health data.
  • We do not sell or share any data — there is nothing to sell or share.

What stays on your device

Everything Kindling remembers lives only on the phone the app is installed on:

  • Your answers to the daily questions and any answer history.
  • Your streak and Kindling flame — the count of days you have connected.
  • Anniversaries and days-together dates you choose to enter.
  • Your settings — appearance, reminder preference, and which packs you have.

This information is saved using Apple's and Google's on-device storage and is never uploaded anywhere. It is deleted when you delete Kindling or clear it yourself inside the app. Because none of it exists on a server, there is no Kindling data of yours that could ever be breached.

No account, no cloud, no sync

Kindling works entirely offline. There is no sign-up, no cloud backup managed by us, and no syncing between devices. The nightly ritual happens on one shared phone, and your answers stay on that phone.

Notifications

Kindling can send an optional gentle nightly reminder if — and only if — you turn that reminder on yourself. These reminders are scheduled locally by your device; no notification content or schedule is sent to us or to any server. If you never enable the reminder, Kindling never asks for notification permission.

Purchases

The optional Pro upgrade (a monthly or yearly subscription, or a one-time lifetime unlock) is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store or by Google through Google Play, using their in-app purchase systems. Kindling never sees your payment method, Apple ID, Google account, or billing details. Those transactions are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy. To restore a purchase, Kindling asks the App Store or Google Play whether this account has already bought Pro — that check is handled by Apple/Google and does not create a Kindling account or reveal your identity to us.

Children

Kindling is intended for couples and is not directed at children under 13. Because Kindling collects no personal data from anyone of any age, no personal information is ever gathered from a child either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes we will update the date at the top of this page. Because the app collects nothing, any change would only ever narrow, not broaden, what we do. Material changes will be noted in the App Store / Google Play release notes.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email dark2torch@gmail.com. We aim to reply within two business days.
