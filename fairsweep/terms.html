3. What FairSweep is (and is not)

FairSweep is a tool for reviewing your own photos and videos and freeing storage on your device. It is not a backup service, a cloud archive, or a recovery tool. FairSweep never deletes anything silently: photos you swipe to remove are only staged inside the app until you review the list and confirm. The actual deletion is performed by Android's own system prompt, and removed items follow Android's standard behaviour (typically moving to your gallery's Trash / Recently Deleted, where they can be recovered for a limited time before space is reclaimed). You are responsible for the photos you choose to delete. We strongly recommend keeping your own backup of anything important before you clean up.
