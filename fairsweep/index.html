Support & FAQ

Need help? Email dark2torch@gmail.com — we aim to reply within two business days. Common questions below.

Are my photos safe?

Yes. FairSweep has no servers and requires no account. Photos are analyzed with Apple's Vision framework entirely on your device and are never uploaded anywhere. Read the full Privacy Policy.

Where do deleted photos go?

Into your Photos app's Recently Deleted album — an Apple safeguard FairSweep cannot bypass. iOS keeps them there for up to 30 days, so you can rescue anything. Your space frees automatically when they expire, or immediately if you empty Recently Deleted yourself (Photos › Albums › Recently Deleted).

Can FairSweep delete something without asking?

No. Swiping left only stages a photo inside the app. You then review the full list, and iOS itself shows a final system confirmation before anything is deleted. Three gates, every time.

Why one-time pricing instead of a subscription?

Because cleaning your camera roll is a utility, not a service. Some cleaner apps charge up to $7.99 per week for a delete button. $9.99 once reflects what it costs to build and maintain the app fairly — without relying on customers forgetting to cancel.

Does it work offline?

Entirely. Swiping, duplicate detection, and deleting all work offline. Your photos are processed on your device, so no connection is needed.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple — visit reportaproblem.apple.com. We have no mechanism to issue refunds directly, and we do not want unhappy customers to pay for something that did not work for them.
