How it plays

Place

Drag turrets from the tray onto the 12-slot ring around your reactor Core.

Merge

Drop a turret on a matching tier to merge it into something meaner. Six tiers deep.

Hold

Waves descend on the Core. Rockets, plasma, lasers — three roles, twelve turrets, ten relics.

Overclock

Burn your burst cooldown at the right moment. Every tenth wave brings the Warden Core.

Purchases are tied to your Apple ID. If you reinstall or switch devices, open the store screen in-game and tap Restore purchases. If it still doesn't appear, email us.
